The Lichess4545 League celebrates 37 successful seasons of weekly classical 45+45 games with over 600 active members, in the faster-paced 15+15 Season 38 Kick-Off Arena on January 6th.

 

With the new year comes new FIDE title regulations. The previously announced FIDE rating changes now have a confirmed effective date of March 1st and will apply to Standard, Rapid and Blitz ratings. The rating changes aim to compress FIDE ratings. This incudes changing the rating floor from 1000 to 1400 and a one time adjustment of players rated under 2000.

FIDE confirmed that GM Alireza Firouzja and GM Koneru Humpy take the final rating spots for the Candidates 2024 and Women's Candidates 2024 respectively. GM Gukesh D joins the field as the runner-up in the FIDE Circuit, since top-ranked GM Fabiano Caruana had already qualified via the World Cup.

 

GM Magnus Carlsen is the FIDE World Blitz Champion 2023, clinching both the World Rapid & Blitz titles as he did last year! GM Valentina Gunina won the Women's World Blitz Championship for the second time after 2012, half a point ahead of GM Alexandra Kosteniuk.
